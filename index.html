Hi, my name is Chriscelle. I was born and raised in the beautiful archipelago of the Philippines and currently living in Canada. I graduated from York University with a Bachelor's Degree in Human Resources Management and currently work as a Technical Recruiter . I envision the future of work as a space of inclusivity, curiosity, and compassion. In addition to my multidimensional self: I am gradually curating myself to become an excellent developer as I am pacing myself through FreeCodeCamp as well as completing George Brown's Certificate of Web Design and Development. I am working towards becoming a future engineer in the tech space where I aspire to build collaborative digital environments for human beings to thrive as a community, more than just independent entities.

If not in front of my laptop or working downtown, you will see me food hopping in the West side of the city, writing about sociological/psychological realizations, playing my keyboards, learning the guitar, playing boardgames, reading a book or strolling anywhere and everywhere to take pictures. I have the travel bug and have been to 11 countries (and still counting). I love to try new cuisines, watch broadway, and take part of history walks to understand different societies.

My ulitmate goal is to live everyday with an intention to build a better tomorrow for myself and the world whether that involves my daily interaction with people and nature or taking a break to energize my mind, body, and soul.
